About DAMPA sa Tabing Dagat

Reserve now to experience an outing and stay at our newly renovated transient houses — perfect for swimming, relaxation, and budget-friendly staycations.

Resort Overview

Summer is waving!! Reserve na po kayo para ma-experience ninyo po ang stay at outing dito sa aming DAMPA sa Tabing Dagat (Newly Renovated - Transient House).

  • 5 Transient Houses — Rooms #1, #2 & #3 are AC rooms (each can accommodate 15 pax sleeping capacity). Rate: ₱7,000
  • Room 4 — Couple room (2–7 pax). Rate: ₱2,500
  • Room 5 (Big Room) — 20 pax capacity. Rate: ₱7,000
  • Each room includes attached open cottage, long table & chairs, CR, sink & faucet (unli water via pressure tank), free use of gas stove & stainless grill.
  • Generator available, sari-sari store on site, tent pitching (rental), bonfire (wood sold on site).

Exclusions & Additional Fees

Fees paid separately by guests on arrival (not included in room rates).

  • Parking Fees (overnight): ₱80 (motor), ₱150 (car/van), ₱200 (jeep). Security attendant on duty.
  • Ecological Fee (barangay): ₱20 per head (adults 8 yrs & above). Children 7 yrs & below exempt. Official receipt issued.
  • Excess Guests: Additional ₱200 per excess person beyond agreed count (adults & kids).
  • Down Payment / Reservation Fee: ₱1,000 (non-refundable) to reserve the slot. First to pay DP secures the date.
